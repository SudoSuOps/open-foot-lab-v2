How it works

One photo a day. A daily read of your foot.

Your daily foot report

Take one photo. On your own device, the OpenFootLab vision model reads it and hands you a short, plain-language report:

What it sees today

Skin, color, spots, drainage, the surgery or amputation site, swelling.

What changed

Measured against your own baseline — your normal is the yardstick, not a stranger's foot.

Where to focus

The areas worth a closer look — and worth bringing to your care team.

Private by design. It runs on your own hardware, in your home — your photos never leave the house, never touch the cloud. It doesn't diagnose and doesn't replace anyone; it helps you notice earlier, and hands your care team a clear place to look.

Your own box

A small device on your desk that is your Foot Passport — it holds your records and runs your daily report, fully private. A serious condition gets a serious tool, the same way a diabetic owns a meter or CGM. You own the machine, you own the record.

Or free, hosted by us

No compute? Use our free hosted model — so nobody is gated from their own daily foot read by hardware cost. We read your photo and hand back the report; we don't keep it. Just want to try it? Start with a phone and a folder.

Freddy — your fren in the room
